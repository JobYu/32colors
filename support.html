Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I save my progress in the coloring game?

Your coloring progress is automatically saved locally in your browser. You can continue your artwork anytime by returning to the same browser. To manually save your progress, click the "üíæ Save Progress" button that appears during gameplay.

My uploaded image doesn't work properly. What should I do?

Please ensure your image meets these requirements:

  • Format: PNG or JPG
  • Maximum size: 300√ó300 pixels
  • Maximum colors: 128 unique colors
  • Clear pixel art style works best

If your image still doesn't work, try reducing the number of colors or resizing it to a smaller dimension.

Can I share my completed artworks?

Yes! Once you complete a coloring artwork, you can use the "üì§ Share Artwork" button to save and share your creation. Your completed artworks are also saved in the "üåü My Gallery" section.

Are my uploaded images stored on your servers?

No, all uploaded images are processed locally in your browser only. We do not upload or store any of your images on our servers. Your privacy and data security are important to us.

The game is running slowly or not loading properly. What can I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Refresh the page and try again
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
  • Ensure your browser is updated to the latest version
  • Try using a smaller image with fewer colors

What browsers are supported?

32colors works best on modern browsers including:

  • Google Chrome (latest version)
  • Mozilla Firefox (latest version)
  • Safari (latest version)
  • Microsoft Edge (latest version)

Can I use this on mobile devices?

Yes! 32colors is designed to work on both desktop and mobile devices. The interface automatically adapts to your screen size for the best experience.
