Privacy Policy

32colors by Number - Pixel Art Coloring Game (Web & Mobile App)

1. Information We Collect

32colors by Number is designed with your privacy in mind. We operate as a client-side application across all platforms, which means:

  • Images: All images you upload are processed locally on your device (browser or mobile app) and are not sent to our servers
  • Game Progress: Your game progress and completed artworks are saved locally on your device (browser storage or app storage)
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information on any platform
  • No Registration Required: You can use all features without creating an account on both web and mobile versions
  • Device Permissions: Mobile app may request storage permissions only to save your artworks locally

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use it for any purpose. The only data processing that occurs is:

  • Converting your uploaded images into coloring games (processed locally)
  • Saving your game progress in your browser's local storage
  • Generating color palettes and game data (all done locally)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using:

  • Web Version: Browser local storage and cache for game settings, progress, and temporary image processing
  • Mobile App: App's local storage and cache for game data, with optional device storage for saving artworks
  • iOS iCloud Sync: iOS app uses iCloud to sync completed level data across your Apple devices for convenience
  • Limited Cloud Storage: Except for iOS iCloud sync of completed levels, we do not store any of your other data on our servers or in the cloud
  • Device Primary: All your data remains primarily on your device

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Web Hosting: To serve the website version
  • App Stores: To distribute the mobile app (Google Play, Apple App Store)
  • Analytics: To understand how our service is used (if implemented, only anonymous usage data)
  • CDN Services: To deliver the website content faster
  • Crash Reporting: To improve app stability (mobile app only, no personal data included)

These services do not have access to your uploaded images or game data on any platform.

5. Cookies and Local Data

We may use local data storage for:

  • Web Version: Cookies and local storage for preferences and essential functionality
  • Mobile App: App preferences and settings stored locally
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data only (if implemented)
  • Game Data: Saving your progress and completed artworks

See our Cookie Policy for more details about web version data usage.

6. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device:

  • Local Processing: Your images never leave your device on any platform
  • No Data Transmission: No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • User Control: You have complete control over your data
  • Data Removal: Web version - clear browser data; Mobile app - uninstall app or clear app data
  • Device Security: Data security depends on your device's security measures

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for children as:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No registration is required
  • All processing is done locally
  • No communication features are present

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Data Deletion: Clear all locally stored data (browser storage or app data)
  • Service Discontinuation: Stop using the service at any time on any platform
  • Content Control: Control what images you upload and process
  • Permission Management: Manage app permissions on mobile devices
  • Export Data: Save your artworks to your device's storage

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
