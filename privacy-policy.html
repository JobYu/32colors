Privacy Policy

32colors by Numbers - Pixel Art Coloring Game

1. Information We Collect

32colors by Numbers is designed with your privacy in mind. We operate as a client-side application, which means:

  • Images: All images you upload are processed locally in your browser and are not sent to our servers
  • Game Progress: Your game progress and completed artworks are saved locally in your browser's storage
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information
  • No Registration Required: You can use all features without creating an account

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, we don't use it for any purpose. The only data processing that occurs is:

  • Converting your uploaded images into coloring games (processed locally)
  • Saving your game progress in your browser's local storage
  • Generating color palettes and game data (all done locally)

3. Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using:

  • Local Storage: For game settings and progress
  • Browser Cache: For temporary processing of images
  • No Server Storage: We do not store any of your data on our servers

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for:

  • Web Hosting: To serve the website
  • Analytics: To understand how our service is used (if implemented, only anonymous usage data)
  • CDN Services: To deliver the website faster

These services do not have access to your uploaded images or game data.

5. Cookies

We may use cookies for:

  • Remembering your preferences
  • Analytics (anonymous usage data only)
  • Essential website functionality

See our Cookie Policy for more details.

6. Data Security

Since all processing happens locally in your browser:

  • Your images never leave your device
  • No transmission of personal data occurs
  • You have complete control over your data
  • Clearing your browser data will remove all stored information

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is safe for children as:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No registration is required
  • All processing is done locally
  • No communication features are present

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear all locally stored data by clearing your browser storage
  • Stop using the service at any time
  • Control what images you upload and process

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.
