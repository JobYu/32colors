Cookie Policy

32colors by Numbers - Pixel Art Coloring Game

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

32colors by Numbers uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Game Progress: To save your coloring progress locally
  • User Preferences: To remember your game settings and preferences
  • Local Storage: To store your completed artworks in your browser

Analytics Cookies (if applicable)

We may use analytics cookies to understand how our website is used:

  • Usage Statistics: To see which features are most popular
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix technical issues
  • Anonymous Data Only: No personal information is collected

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration Required
Local Storage Save game progress and completed artworks Until manually cleared Yes
Session Storage Temporary game data during your session Until browser closes Yes
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 1 year No
Analytics Cookies Anonymous usage statistics 2 years No

4. Local Storage

Our website primarily uses browser Local Storage instead of traditional cookies for:

  • Storing your game progress
  • Keeping your completed artworks
  • Remembering your game preferences
  • Caching processed image data

Local Storage data remains on your device until you manually clear it or uninstall your browser.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Web Analytics: If we use analytics services (like Google Analytics)
  • CDN Services: For faster content delivery
  • Hosting Services: For website functionality

These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie policies.

6. Managing Cookies

You have several options to manage cookies:

Browser Settings

  • Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
  • You can set your browser to notify you when cookies are being set
  • You can choose to accept only essential cookies

Clearing Local Storage

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear Browsing Data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Clear Browsing Data

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies or clear local storage:

  • Your game progress will not be saved
  • Completed artworks will not be stored
  • Your preferences will not be remembered
  • Some website features may not work properly

8. Cookie Consent

By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. Since we primarily use essential cookies for basic functionality, your continued use of the site constitutes consent.

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our use of cookies, please contact us through our website.
